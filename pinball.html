Privacy policy — Crown Pinball

Last updated: 2026-05-25

Plain-language summary

Crown Pinball does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. It is a fully offline game. There are no ads, no analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs, no servers, and no internet permission requested.

What data does the app collect?

None. The app does not collect any personal information, usage statistics, device identifiers, location data, or any other data about you or your device.

What about my high score?

Your high score is stored locally on your device using Android's DataStore. It never leaves your watch. Uninstalling the app deletes it.

Does the app use the internet?

No. The app does not declare any internet permission and cannot make network requests. It runs entirely offline.

What permissions does the app use, and why?

  • VIBRATE (haptic feedback on bumper hits)

We do not use any other permissions. No camera, microphone, contacts, location, body sensors (beyond what's listed above, if any), calendar, SMS, or storage.

Third parties

We do not use any third-party services, SDKs, advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash reporters, or A/B testing services.

Children's privacy

The game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. (And again — we don't collect any data at all.)

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new version at the same URL.
